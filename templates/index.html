About SS Labs

SS Labs is a research and deployment group dedicated to making advanced language models accessible and efficient. We focus on converting open-source LLMs to optimized GGUF formats, enabling fine-tuning on consumer-grade hardware, and building multilingual text generation solutions.

  • Build multilingual text generation models
  • Enable deployment on various platforms and cloud providers
